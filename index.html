About Us

Prasanna Electrical Engineering Services was founded in 2021 by our Director, Mr. Bhushan R. Thorat. We are a technology-driven company and a registered "A" Class Electrical Contractor. Specializing in electrical contracting, we undertake all types of electrical projects and turnkey solutions for industries, commercial complexes, banks, IT parks, shopping malls, and government organizations. Additionally, we provide maintenance services for electrical equipment and installations.


With a team of highly trained professionals and extensive experience in LT and HT electrical installations, we ensure reliable and efficient service delivery.


Our Solar Solutions

We have also expanded into the solar energy sector, offering comprehensive solar solutions such as: On-Grid, Off-Grid, Hybrid-Grid, Solar Water Pump, Solar Street Light With CCTV etc.

These services are provided under government subsidy schemes, ensuring top-quality solutions that promote renewable energy adoption.


Our Clients & Projects

We take pride in serving esteemed clients like MSEDCL Co., Zilla Parishad Nashik, and PWD, executing APDRP, infrastructure, and turnkey projects across Maharashtra. Our expertise extends to private sectors, builders, and industries, delivering high-quality and efficient electrical solutions.


Our Growth

As of March 31, 2024, our firm has successfully achieved a turnover of ₹50 lakh, with a projected sales target of ₹1 crore for the financial year 2024–2025.

We look forward to collaborating with you and assure you of our commitment to excellence, timely execution, and quality service.
